What we do

Engineering for every part of the structure

From the footing design under a new home to strengthening an existing building, Centraus covers the structural side of your project end to end.

01

Structural design

Beams, bracing, portals and connections for new homes, extensions and light-commercial builds — sized, detailed and ready to construct.

02

Foundation & footing design

Slabs, piles and footings designed for Canterbury's TC2 and TC3 ground, with geotech coordination where the site calls for it.

03

Seismic assessment & strengthening

Initial and detailed seismic assessments (ISA/DSA) and practical strengthening schemes for existing buildings and earthquake-affected structures.

04

Producer statements (PS1–PS4)

Design and construction-review producer statements to satisfy building consent, plus on-site reviews through the construction phase.

05

Retaining walls & decks

Engineered retaining walls, decks and structural landscaping — designed for the loads, drainage and consent your site actually needs.

06

Remedial & repair engineering

Investigation and repair design for cracked slabs, sagging structures and weathertightness or earthquake damage — clear advice on what's required.

About Centraus

Structural advice you can build on

Centraus Structural Consulting is a Christchurch engineering practice working alongside builders, architects, developers and homeowners across Canterbury. We handle the structural side of a project so the rest of the team can keep moving — sound design, clear documentation, and the certification council needs.

Whether it's a single beam for a renovation or the full structure and foundations of a new build, our focus is the same: practical engineering that's buildable on site and stands up to council scrutiny without the back-and-forth.

  • Locally owned and Christchurch based
  • Clear fixed scope and pricing before we start
  • Documentation prepared for Christchurch City Council consent
