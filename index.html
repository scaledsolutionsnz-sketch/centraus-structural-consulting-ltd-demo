What we do

Structural engineering, start to consent

Whether you're a homeowner adding a room, a builder needing a producer statement, or an architect after design support — here's how we help.

01

Residential structural design

New builds, additions, alterations and removing that wall — beams, lintels, bracing and connections sized and detailed for the build.

02

Producer statements (PS1 & PS4)

Design producer statements for your consent and construction review on site, so council has the sign-off it needs.

03

Foundations & retaining walls

Slabs, footings, piles and timber or block retaining designed for your site's ground conditions and slope.

04

Seismic assessments

Detailed and initial seismic assessments for existing buildings, plus strengthening design where it's needed.

05

Commercial & light industrial

Portal frames, mezzanines, steel framing and fit-outs for commercial spaces, sheds and warehouses.

06

Consent documentation

Structural calculations, drawings, specifications and reports packaged up ready to lodge with your building consent.

About us

Engineering you can build from

Centraus Structural Consulting is a Christchurch structural engineering practice working alongside homeowners, builders and architects across Canterbury. We take your concept or architectural plans and work out how it actually stands up.

The goal is simple: a design that's safe and practical to build, documentation that gets through consent without a pile of RFIs, and an engineer who picks up the phone when your builder has a question on site.
