Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Centraus Structural Consulting LTD respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect through this website and how we use it, consistent with the Privacy Act 2020.

What we collect

When you send an enquiry or feedback, we collect the details you provide — typically your name, phone number, email, suburb and a description of your project. We don't ask for more than we need to respond to you.

How we use it

  • To reply to your enquiry and prepare a scope and quote.
  • To carry out engineering work you engage us for.
  • To keep records of our correspondence with you.

We don't sell your information, and we don't send marketing you didn't ask for.

Form handling

Our website forms are delivered by a third-party form service (FormSubmit), which passes your message to us by email. Your details are handled to deliver that message and are subject to that provider's own terms.

Storage and sharing

We keep enquiry information only as long as we reasonably need it. We don't share it with third parties except where it's necessary to deliver our services (for example a council or another consultant on your project) or where the law requires it.

Your choices

You can ask to see the personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it where we're not required to keep it. Just get in touch.

Contact

For any privacy question, call 021 356751 or use the enquiry form.
