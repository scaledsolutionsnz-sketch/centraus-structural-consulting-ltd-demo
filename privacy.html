1. Our commitment

Centraus Structural Consulting LTD respects your privacy and handles personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 2020. This policy explains what we collect through this website and how we use it.

2. Information we collect

When you submit an enquiry or feedback form, we collect the information you provide — typically your name, email address, phone number, suburb and details about your project. We do not collect more than we need to respond to you.

  • Contact details you enter into our forms
  • Details of your project or enquiry
  • Any feedback you choose to share with us

3. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to respond to your enquiry, prepare scopes and fee proposals, deliver our engineering services, and keep in touch about your project. We do not sell your information.

4. Form handling

Enquiry and feedback forms on this website are processed through a third-party form delivery service (FormSubmit) which forwards your message to our email. Their handling of data is subject to their own privacy terms.

5. Disclosure

We only share your information where necessary to deliver our services (for example with a geotechnical engineer or the relevant council on your project), where you have asked us to, or where required by law.

6. Storage and security

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure and to protect it from loss, misuse or unauthorised access. We retain it only as long as needed for the purposes described above or as required by law.

7. Your rights

You may request access to, or correction of, the personal information we hold about you. To do so, contact us using the details below.

8. Contact

For any privacy questions or requests, contact michael.king@centraus.co.nz or call 021 356751.
